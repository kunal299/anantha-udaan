Achievements

Small actions. Lasting change. At Anantha Udaan, every step we take is student-led and community-rooted.

Dastavezikaran

Legal Identity, financial inclusion and awareness

  • Families are educated on schemes, entitlements, and informed decision-making.
  • Facilitated birth certificates for 10+ children and Aadhaar/ration cards for 10+ families, ensuring legal identity.
  • Helped 30+ families open joint bank accounts, enabling financial identity, savings access, and long-term stability.

Operation Pahal and Breakfast Initiative

  • Volunteers escort children to school in Chittupur and Bhagwanpur.
  • Weekly felicitation sessions reward attendance and academic effort.
  • Daily nutritious breakfast provided to about 10 children at the Bhagwanpur site.
  • Supports children’s physical, mental and academic well-being.

Competitive Division

  • Basic Siksha Adhikari(BSA granting permission for collaboration with Primary school near BHU)
  • School going students have achieved NIPUN target set by Government of India

Skill Development

Vasudha products

  • Launching of Vasudha products made by women from local communities, contributing to:
    • Economic empowerment
    • Enhancing autonomy
    • Enhancing social recognition and upliftment
